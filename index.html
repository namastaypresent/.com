Mindful Meditation


Susan suffered most of her life with anxiety and struggled to be present and live in the moment. Eleven years ago she discovered yoga and meditation. These practices changed her life. She became inspired to help others so she got trained and certified in Meditation, Sound Therapy, Yoga Instruction, Holistic Nutrition and Reiki. Attending these classes will help reduce stress, anxiety, and depression. You will leave feeling more focused, relaxed, peaceful, happy and grounded.

Our classes are offered in 15 min, half hour, 45 minutes or one hour intervals before work, at lunch time or after work. They can also be customized and include sage cleansing, breath work, essential oils, sound therapy, Reiki, guided meditation, meditation music, and yoga style stretching.
